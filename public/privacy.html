Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This privacy policy describes how Runes by Vilhelm (runesbyvilhelm.com), operated by Vilhelm Westman, handles personal data. Your privacy matters and only the minimum necessary data is collected.

Who is responsible

Vilhelm Westman is the data controller for this website. You can reach him via the contact form on the site.

Data we collect

This website does not use tracking cookies or analytics. Personal data is only collected when you voluntarily submit the commission enquiry form. The form collects:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Details about your commission request (motif, size, colours, message)

Form submissions are processed by Formspree and forwarded by email to Vilhelm Westman. Formspree may store your submission on their servers. See Formspree's privacy policy for details.

How we use your data

Information submitted through the contact form is used solely to respond to your enquiry and, if agreed, to fulfil a commission. Your data is never sold, shared with third parties for marketing, or used for any other purpose.

Third-party services

This site uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts – fonts are loaded from Google's servers, which may log your IP address. See Google's privacy policy.
  • Formspree – handles contact form submissions.
  • GitHub Pages – hosts this website. GitHub may collect basic server logs. See GitHub's privacy statement.

Cookies

This website does not set any cookies. Google Fonts may set cookies via their CDN; these are outside our control.

Data retention

Email correspondence related to commissions is retained for as long as necessary to fulfil the commission and for a reasonable period thereafter for accounting purposes. You may request deletion at any time.

Your rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact Vilhelm Westman via the contact form on the site.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated occasionally. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
